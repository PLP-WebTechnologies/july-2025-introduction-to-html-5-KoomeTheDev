Introduction to Semantic HTML5

Semantic HTML5 provides meaningful elements that describe the structure and content of a web page, improving accessibility and SEO.

Why Accessibility and SEO Matter

Building accessible websites ensures everyone can use your content, including people with disabilities. SEO helps search engines understand your content better, boosting visibility.

Accessibility Features

  • Proper use of landmarks and headings
  • Descriptive link text
  • Use of ARIA roles where needed

SEO Basics

  • Meaningful titles and meta descriptions
  • Logical heading hierarchy
  • Readable, descriptive content
